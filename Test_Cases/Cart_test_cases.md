# TC-013 – Add product to cart

**Preconditions:**  
- User is on the products page

**Test Steps:**  
1. Click "Add to Cart" for a product

**Expected Result:**  
- Product is added to the cart  
- Cart icon shows updated quantity


# TC-014 – Remove product from cart

**Preconditions:**  
- User has at least one product in the cart

**Test Steps:**  
1. Open the cart  
2. Click "Remove" on a product

**Expected Result:**  
- Product is removed from the cart  
- Cart quantity updates correctly
