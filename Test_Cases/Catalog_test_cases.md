# Product List / Catalog – Test Cases

## TC-008 – Display of product list

**Preconditions:**  
- User is logged in
- User is on the products page

**Test Steps:**
1. Open the products page

**Expected Result:**
- Product list is displayed
- Products are shown without errors or empty states


## TC-009 – Product basic information visibility

**Preconditions:**  
- User is on the products page

**Test Steps:**
1. View any product in the product list

**Expected Result:**
- Product image is displayed
- Product name is displayed
- Product price is displayed


## TC-010 – Open product details page

**Preconditions:**  
- User is on the products page

**Test Steps:**
1. Click on a product name or image

**Expected Result:**
- Product details page opens successfully


## TC-011 – Product details information correctness

**Preconditions:**  
- Product details page is open

**Test Steps:**
1. Review product information on the details page

**Expected Result:**
- Product name is displayed correctly
- Product description is displayed
- Product price is displayed
- Product image is displayed


## TC-012 – Product details page navigation

**Preconditions:**  
- Product details page is open

**Test Steps:**
1. Click the "Back" button or use browser back navigation

**Expected Result:**
- User is returned to the products page
